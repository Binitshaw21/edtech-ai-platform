Quick Start Guide

1. How do I transcribe a YouTube video?

Go to your Dashboard and click te "Paste Link" tab. Paste any standard YouTube URL and click "Extract Audio". Our backend will automatically download the video, extract the audio, and transcribe it using Whisper AI. This process usually takes 10-30 seconds depending on the video length.

2. How do I generate Flashcards & Quizzes?

Once you have a transcribed note in your dashboard, click the glowing "Generate AI Study Pack" button on that specific note. Google Gemini will analyze the text and create a summary, flashcards, and a multiple-choice quiz. Note: This works best on educational lectures!

3. Is my data secure?

Yes. All passwords are cryptographically hashed using Bcrypt before being stored in our MongoDB database. Your voice recordings and temporary video files are permanently deleted from our servers the exact second the AI finishes transcribing them.
